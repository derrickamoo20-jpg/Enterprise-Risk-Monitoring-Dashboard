# Enterprise Risk Monitoring Dashboard

## Overview
This project demonstrates how enterprise risks can be tracked, prioritized, and monitored using Power BI. It combines a structured risk register with interactive visuals to help decision-makers understand risk exposure, monitor mitigation progress, and identify overdue actions.

## Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to track risks consistently across business units. Without a centralized view, it becomes difficult to identify high-risk areas, monitor accountability, and ensure mitigation plans are progressing on time.

## Solution
I built an interactive Enterprise Risk Monitoring Dashboard in Power BI using a structured risk register dataset. The dashboard provides:
- Total risk count
- Overdue risk tracking
- Open risk monitoring
- High-risk identification
- Risk concentration heatmap
- Mitigation status analysis
- Interactive filtering by status, risk owner, and business unit

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX

## Key Features
- KPI cards for Total Risks, Overdue Risks, Open Risks, and High Risks
- Bar chart showing where the biggest risk categories are
- Stacked chart showing mitigation status by risk category
- Risk heatmap using likelihood and impact
- Interactive slicers for Status, Risk Owner, and Business Unit

## Key Insights
- The dashboard highlights concentration of risks in medium-high impact and likelihood zones
- High-risk items can be quickly identified and monitored
- Overdue risks can be isolated for immediate escalation
- Mitigation progress can be compared across risk categories
